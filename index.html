About Me

Born and raised in Maryland, I am happy to call Baltimore home. I stayed local for college and attended the University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC) where I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in both Visual Arts and Media Communications. When I first arrived at UMBC, I was initially interested in Animation, but later developed a strong interest in Graphic Design and made that my primary focus. I have always had a great interest in sports and had the honor of playing varsity lacrosse at UMBC. It was a great four year experience — the team won three American East Championships and made four trips to the NCAA Tournament.

My professional career began as a web designer with a small branding agency in Ellicott City, Maryland called Galley Creative Group. Here I was able to fine-tuned my skills establishing strong web presences for clients. Early in my career, I was able to work comprehensively with several powerful content management systems, including Magento, Drupal, Joomla and WordPress. My early interraction with these open-source platforms inspired me to learn more and expand my development knowledge.

In 2011, I made the transition to Unleashed Technologies. At Unleashed I have continued to expand my skillset and have worked on some really exicting projects. Working with the talented team at Unleashed Technologies has helped me grow as a professional and really fine tune my skills. My focus over the past five years has been design and development for Drupal and Magento projects, but there have been WordPress and Symphony projects mixed in as well.
