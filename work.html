Work

I have had the priviledge to work with some fantastic clients and brands over the past few years from a wide range of industries. Below are a few sample projects that I'm proud of. More process details and design artifacts can be made available by request.

SCORE

Since 1964, SCORE has helped more than 11 million entrepreneurs start, grow or successfully exit a business. We helped SCORE reimagine their digital experience to better support their mentors, partners, and visitors.

MyOchsner app

Phase2 partnered with Ochsner Health to evolve their mobile approach and strategy. The result—the MyOchsner app. This new app improves the consumer experience, increases engagement, and expands the health system’s digital reach.

Michigan Medicine

Michigan Medicine is a leader in life-saving medicine, delivering excellence in clinical care, research, and education. As the design lead with the Phase2 team, we collaborated with Michigan Medicine to establish a unified digital presence — pulling together their enterpirse, clinical, research, and education web properties into one cohesive system. Our team provided them with a flexible component library to enable them to build consistent and engaging experiences across the entire framework.

Gundersen Health

GHS came to Phase2 for the design and development of their new unified digital front door — a collection of platforms housed under one system. We collaborated with their team to establish a new design system that brought them brand consistency, flexibility, and impact.

NAFSA: Association of International Educators

At the end of the project, NAFSA had a new flexible website that allowed their passion for international education to shine through. Visitors are more engaged and can more easily find pertinent content, while administrators have a platform that can easily update website content for those visitors.

National Guard Association (NGAUS)

Air Force Magazine

Through our redesign efforts to migrate Air Force Magazine from SharePoint to WordPress, we doubled their web traffic and increased reader engagement. The magazine has recently rebranded to Air & Space Forces Magazine but has maintained the interface design.

Sandy Spring Bank

Sandy Spring Bank, headquartered in Olney, MD, has over 50 locations across Maryland, Virginia, and Washington, DC. As the lead designer with Unleashed Technologies, I collaborated with the SSB team to establish a streamlined website information architecture based on their various service lines and offerings. While small elements of the original design have been adjusted over time, the core theme has been a strong foundation for quite some time.
